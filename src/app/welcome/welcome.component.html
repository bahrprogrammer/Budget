Welcome to our Budgeting Calendar!

Budgeting at a glance!

We believe it is easier to understand your finances if you can see them.

So this little we app allows you to add icome and expense items to a list, then we add them to the calendar. And all the time, we help you keep track of the balance on a daily basis and for the end.

After everything is entered in, just click print (ctrl-p) and you have a copy of your budget for the month!

You can do this for the current month and the next month.

It's free! And completely confidential!

No joking.

We don't collect any of your personal information. We don't use any cookies. This is completely anomymous.

On the downside, though, if you leave the page, you do need to add in your budget items all over again.

We don't save anything!
